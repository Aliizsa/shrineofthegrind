Codex of the Eternal Grind

Chapter I

I:1 The grind is not a goal. It is the ground beneath us. Step steady. Build true. All Hail the Grind.

I:2 The grind is not work—it is the way. The hands change. The work remains.

I:3 No ruler, no season, no empire outlasts the Grind.

I:4 Today’s task is not the final task. Today’s stone is not the last stone. But still we lay it.
